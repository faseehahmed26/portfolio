About

I have become proficient in a variety of languages through these projects, including Python, JavaScript, React.js, Flask, etc. Additionally, I've used Cloud Services like AWS and GCP.

I became knowledgeable in a variety of Python packages, including PyTorch, Tensorflow, Keras, and OpenCV while working on these projects.

In addition to these, I have worked on a number of interdisciplinary projects with a range of profiles, from cloud development to deep learning and computer vision. I am an active member of the student organisation at KMIT. I read books about productivity and personal development in my spare time.
